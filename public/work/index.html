Black Fax off the Hook

Doza gallery, Sofia. Part of SPorno festival 'THE DEVIANTS'
May 28 — June 1, 2024

An insurrectionary installation at Doza: a hacked fax machine endlessly prints AI-made gothic fan-fiction, skewering media overload and Bulgarian populism.

Postcards and Contraband

Charta Gallery, Posta Space
Sofia, Bulgaria
April 11 — May 12, 2024

The exhibition Postcards and Contraband by artist Nikola Stoyanov is an investigation of a work archive of the Bulgarian Customs Agency, Directorate for Combating Drug Trafficking from 1975-1985.

Undisclosed location

Nature Morte
