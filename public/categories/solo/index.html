selected exhibitions

Postcards and Contraband

Charta Gallery, Posta Space
Sofia, Bulgaria
April 11 — May 12, 2024

The exhibition Postcards and Contraband by artist Nikola Stoyanov is an investigation of a work archive of the Bulgarian Customs Agency, Directorate for Combating Drug Trafficking from 1975-1985.

Nature Morte

Goethe-Institut, Sofia
June 22 — July 30, 2023

The exhibition Nature Morte by artist Nikola Stoyanov is a multimedia project dealing with the concept of geotrauma in the context of the Martisa Mining complex.

Latent Space

Doza Gallery, Sofia
September 8 — September 21, 2022

A is where the gaze of artificial intelligence itself draws a picturesque horizon, delving into the hidden rooms of secret research facilities, investigative organizations, classified databases.

Re:Process

Arhis Gallery, Varna
May 21 — June 5, 2022

Photographs of everyday urban objects. In the construction and deconstruction traces can be found upon the urban surface, a peculiar language, through which different modalities of appearance are formed and unfolded.

I-Ixa

Sofia, Bulgaria

The exhibition is nomadic in nature, led by the black sea, which is landlocked and shoreless, far from the clear croak of the sea bird.

In:Process

KO-OP Gallery
Sofia, Bulgaria

The drive to capture the city as an affective totality

Music for a New Night Life

Talyan Art Fest, Varna
June 8 — July 9, 2019

A cymatic matrix of a city in flux, of its dreams and nightmares and finally of its reality filled with nostalgia.
